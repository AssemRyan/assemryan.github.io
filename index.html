Automation Project

Nuevo 35 Test Bench Automation

Improvement of the stop behavior of a test-bench control program using NI LabVIEW.

Initial Situation

Pressing Stop immediately terminated the program without a controlled deceleration, creating a potential safety risk.

Goal

Implement a smooth, user-defined shutdown sequence instead of an abrupt stop.

Implemented Solution

The LabVIEW logic was extended with a ramp-down sequence, controlled voltage calculations, stop-state handling and a restart lockout during deceleration.

Result

The test bench now shuts down to zero within a configurable time, while restart is only permitted after complete standstill.

Project gallery

Key outcomes
  • Controlled shutdown to zero within a user-defined time.
  • Correct voltage calculations maintained during deceleration.
  • Restart blocked until the test bench reaches complete standstill.

Result and demonstration

The video shows the controlled ramp-down sequence and confirms that restart remains blocked until the test bench reaches complete standstill.

Bachelor's Thesis

3D Printer with Integrated Laser Engraver

Design and development of a dual-purpose prototype that combines FDM additive manufacturing and laser engraving on a shared motion system.

Project Objective

Develop one compact platform capable of producing polymer parts and engraving flat workpieces without requiring two separate machines.

Mechanical Development

The work covered the machine structure, linear motion, build area and integration of the printing and laser-processing functions.

Testing & Calibration

Functional trials were used to adjust motion, process settings, nozzle positioning and laser focus for repeatable operation.

Result

A working prototype demonstrated both manufacturing modes and provided a practical basis for evaluating accuracy and usability.

Development and results

Project focus
  • Mechanical design and system integration.
  • FDM printing and laser-engraving process trials.
  • Prototype calibration, testing and result evaluation.
